# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Borgo Pumps is used by farm owners and farm workers/operators who need to control agricultural pumps from a phone. They may be standing outdoors, working in bright sunlight, moving between fields, or checking pump status quickly while handling other farm tasks. The interface must support fast decisions, clear device status, and low-friction control for both technical and non-technical users.

## Product Purpose

Borgo Pumps helps users register GSM-based pump controllers, organize pumps into groups, schedule ON/OFF operations, and review recent activity. Success means a user can confidently understand what is running, stop or start pumps safely, and verify that commands were sent or acknowledged.

## Brand Personality

Modern, friendly, and dependable. Borgo Pumps should feel approachable enough for everyday farm work while still looking like serious operational software. The product should communicate calm control, clear status, and trust in remote pump operations.

## Anti-references

The interface should not look like a generic green farming app, a childish agriculture illustration app, an overly corporate enterprise dashboard, a crypto/fintech trading dashboard, or a harsh industrial control panel. It should avoid decorative farm clichés and instead feel like a clean mobile control room for real field operations.

## Design Principles

1. Make machine state unmistakable.
2. Keep critical actions reachable but deliberate.
3. Use friendly language without weakening operational clarity.
4. Prioritize outdoor readability and large touch targets.
5. Design for future sensors and automation without overloading the MVP.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target good mobile accessibility with high contrast, readable text, large tap targets, clear labels, visible states, and layouts that remain usable outdoors. Avoid color-only status communication; pair color with text labels. Motion should be subtle and non-blocking.
